Podcaster

Home / Job / Podcaster

Job Description

We’re looking for someone who’s willing to take on many different roles while creating cohesive and addictive shows. The job is super hands on. You’ll help shape stories, book interviews, prep and outsource research, and work with a team to create sparkling audio. A typical week will include working on a number of shows in various stages of production. You should be detail-oriented, deadline-driven, growth-minded, and comfortable working in both a team and independently. You’ll have an eye for finding a compelling angle for any given subject, and an ability to tell a story both conversationally and creatively.

Application assignment

  • Create a 5 minute native and original podcast in any area of interest.
  • Post your podcast on your Instagram, Facebook & Twitter accounts using the hashtag #NRGeticMVPs.
  • Copy link of your video as posted on Instagram/Facebook/Twitter and submit on the next page
  • Encourage as many people as possible to like, comment and follow your progress on our social media platforms @nrgtv on YouTube, @nrgradiokenya Facebook and @nrgradiokenya on Twitter/Instagram.
